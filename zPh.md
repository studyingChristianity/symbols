## Ideal Saliva / Oral pH

| Age Range        | ph Level                                                 |
| ---------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Children**     | 6.5 – 7.5                                                             |
| **Adults**       | 6.2 – 7.6                                                             |
| **Older adults** | 6.2 – 7.6 (may trend slightly acidic if dry mouth increases with age) |
